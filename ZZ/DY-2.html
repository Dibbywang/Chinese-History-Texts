DY-2

Written: 2024-9-28

Second year, Spring, Duke saw the people of Rong at Qian.
Summer, May, the people of Ju went to the place of Xiang.
Wu Hai led an army and invaded the place called Ji (极).
Autumn, August, seventeenth, the Duke and Rong conducted an alliance at the place called Tang.
September, the country of Ji (纪) sent an official called Lie Ru to take woman that will be married to their duke.
Winter, October, Bo Ji, this woman, left for the country of Ji.
Ji and Ju conducted an alliance at the place called Mi.
December, Zhong Zi died.
The people of Zheng invaded the country of Wei.

Second year, Spring, the Duke met the people of Rong at the place of Qian. This is because the Duke is extending the good relationship established by the previous Duke Hui. The people of Rong asked for an alliance, but it is rejected by the Duke.
Ju asked the country of Xiang to conduct an marriage. The woman Jiang did not want to be the victim of an arranged marriage, so she returned to her country without the consent of her husband. In the summer, the people of Ju invaded Xiang and forcefully took Jiang back.
Si Kong of Lu (a high military position) Wu Hai invaded the place of Ji (极), Fei Qin Fu lead the Lu army to victory.
Rong asked for an alliance, which is conducted in Autumn at the place of Tang. This is to continue the good relationships between Lu and Rong.
September, Lie Ru of Ji (纪) came to take the woman that will be married to their duke. He is doing this as ordered by his duke.
Winter, Ji Lie Ru and the duke of Ju conducted an alliance at the place of Mi. This is because the Duke of Lu asked them to.
The invasion of Wei by Zheng is because of the previous invasion of Zheng by Wei on Gong Shu Hua's behalf.
